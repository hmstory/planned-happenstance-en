What is Planned Happenstance?

Planned Happenstance is a career development theory that says chance events play a significant role in our careers. Instead of rigid planning, we should learn to recognize and capitalize on unexpected opportunities.

The theory identifies 5 key skills that help turn unplanned events into career opportunities: Curiosity, Persistence, Flexibility, Optimism, and Risk-taking.
